From Resi to Commercial Solar: A Solar Industry Perspective

solarcommercial solarcareerpodcast

I recently had the pleasure of joining Joe Ordia on the Solar Surge Podcast to discuss my journey in the solar industry and share insights about the transition from residential to commercial solar.

The commercial and industrial (C&I) solar market represents one of the biggest opportunities in clean energy today. While residential solar gets much of the media attention, C&I solar is where the real megawatts are deployed — and where experienced solar professionals can make the biggest impact.

Key Takeaways

  • The C&I solar market requires a fundamentally different sales approach than residential
  • Relationship building and technical credibility are essential for success
  • Understanding utility rate structures and financial modeling is critical
  • The IRA and ITC incentives have created unprecedented opportunities in C&I

If you're considering making the leap from residential to commercial solar, the timing has never been better. The market is growing, the incentives are strong, and the demand for experienced professionals is high.
