After 30+ years in technology and sustainability, I've never been more optimistic about the future of clean energy. The convergence of three technologies — solar PV, battery storage, and artificial intelligence — is creating possibilities that would have seemed like science fiction just a decade ago.

## The Solar Revolution is Real

Solar is no longer an alternative energy source — it's the cheapest form of new electricity generation in most of the world. The cost curve continues to decline, and deployment continues to accelerate. But solar's intermittency challenge has long been its Achilles heel.

## Storage Changes Everything

Battery energy storage systems (BESS) are the missing piece that makes solar a true baseload power source. With LFP battery costs dropping below $200/kWh and heading lower, the economics of solar + storage are becoming irresistible for commercial and industrial customers.

## AI is the Multiplier

Artificial intelligence amplifies the impact of both solar and storage. AI optimizes when batteries charge and discharge, predicts maintenance needs before they become failures, and manages complex portfolios of distributed energy resources as if they were a single power plant.

## The Convergence

When you combine cheap solar, affordable storage, and intelligent management, you get something genuinely new: distributed, resilient, affordable clean energy that works for everyone — not just early adopters.
