# Copy Audit & Changes Log

**Audit Date:** March 19, 2026
**Purpose:** Document all copy modifications made during the site rebuild, with rationale for each change.

---

## Methodology

All copy was audited against these criteria:
1. Clarity and conciseness for C-suite audience
2. SEO keyword integration (solar consulting, cleantech, NABCEP, energy storage)
3. Consistent brand voice (authoritative, approachable, industry-expert)
4. Accurate statistics and credentials
5. Clear calls-to-action

---

## Page-by-Page Changes

### Homepage (`/`)

| Element | Original (Wix) | New | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hero headline | "Clean Power Consulting Group" | "Grow Your Cleantech Business with Confidence" | Action-oriented, benefit-focused |

## Global Copy Standards Applied

- **Credentials format:** "30+ years of experience" (not "over thirty years")
- **Company name:** "Clean Power Consulting Group" (formal), "Clean Power Hour" (podcast only)
- **Tim's title:** "President & Founder"
- **Statistics always include source context** (e.g., "150+ MW of solar developed")
- **CTAs use action verbs:** "Book Your Strategy Call," "Explore Services," "View Course"
